Data Insights for Anti-Drug Campaigns - Demographics Analysis

Based on Data on Accidental Drug-Related Deaths in Connecticut 2012 - 2018

You are probably going to need a wider screen for this visualization
A basic analysis of the data by sex reveals that about 75% of deaths affected the Male demographic compared to the Female demographic. It also reveals that, generally, Opioids caused more deaths. Hovering over the different bars in the visualization will reveal the exact number of deaths for a particular drug and demographic.
