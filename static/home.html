Data Insights for Anti-Drug Campaigns

Based on Data on Accidental Drug-Related Deaths in Connecticut 2012 - 2018

You are probably going to need a wider screen for this visualization
There were several drug related accidental deaths reported in the state of Connecticut from 2012 to 2018. These deaths were very varied in nature. They were caused by different drugs and affected different demographics. This visualization displays the drugs that caused the most accidental fatalities by numbers in that time frame.
Data Demographic Insights

More targeted and accurate analysis of this data may help with tailoring anti-drug campaigns to suit different the demographics in a population, like in the State of Connecticut. Hence, make the campaigns more relevant and efficient.
